📚 Textos para Comprobar Nivel de Inglés - Clase Inducción
Instrucciones al Teacher
  1. El profesor debe preguntar al estudiante en qué nivel cree que está.
  2. Debe poner el audio de la lectura correspondiente al nivel que el estudiante escogió y pedirle que explique lo que entendió del audio. Si el estudiante no es capaz de entender al menos el 95% del contenido, aún no está en ese nivel.
  3. Debe poner la lectura del nivel que el estudiante escogió y pedirle que lea el texto en voz alta. Si el estudiante no es capaz de pronunciar correctamente al menos el 95% de la lectura, aún no está en ese nivel.
  4. Debe poner la lectura del nivel que el estudiante escogió y pedirle que traduzca el texto. Si el estudiante no es capaz de traducir correctamente al menos el 95% del contenido, aún no está en ese nivel.
  5. Si el estudiante no pasa las tres pruebas anteriores en su totalidad, no está apto para aprobar ese nivel. El profesor debe retroceder al nivel anterior y realizar las mismas pruebas, y así sucesivamente.

1. My Family

4. My Favorite Color
My favorite color is blue. I like blue because it is calm and peaceful. My room is blue, and I also have a blue backpack. I think blue is a beautiful color for the sky and the ocean. I also like green because it reminds me of nature.


5. My Weekend
On weekends, I like to relax. On Saturday, I go to the park with my family. We play soccer and walk around. On Sunday, I stay at home and read books or watch TV. Sometimes, I visit my grandparents. I enjoy spending time with them.

During my studies, I learned a lot about managing projects, working in teams, and solving real-world business problems. I also participated in internships that gave me hands-on experience.

I am very concerned about the environment. Climate change is a global issue that affects everyone. I believe that it is important to reduce our carbon footprint by using renewable energy, recycling, and reducing waste. Small changes in our daily lives, like using less plastic and conserving water, can have a big impact on the environment. Governments and companies also need to take action to protect our planet.


4. My Career Aspirations
I am currently working in the marketing department of a large company. My goal is to advance in my career and take on more responsibility. I am also considering further education, perhaps pursuing a master’s degree in business or digital marketing. In the future, I would like to start my own business and help other companies with their marketing strategies.


5. The Future of Technology
Technology is advancing at a rapid pace, and I believe it will continue to shape our future in profound ways. Artificial intelligence, for example, is already changing industries such as healthcare, finance, and transportation. In the next few years, we may see even more innovation in areas like virtual reality and automation.
