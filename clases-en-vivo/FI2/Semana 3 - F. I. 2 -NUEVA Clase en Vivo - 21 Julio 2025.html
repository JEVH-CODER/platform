PREGUNTAS - DEBATE
PREGUNTAS: Historia Angélica (Más avanzado)

Todos los estudiantes deben preparar sus respuestas para las preguntas:

Chapter 1: The Diagnosis

  1. How does Angélica react to the news of her diagnosis?
  2. Why do you think Angélica decides to stay strong and focus on her family rather than giving in to fear?
  3. What would you do if you received news that completely changed your future? Would you make plans, or would you feel overwhelmed?
    Chapter 2: A Final Wish
  4. Why does Angélica ask her children to let her eat whatever she wants?
  5. How does Angélica’s faith influence her mindset in the face of her diagnosis?
  6. What would you do if someone close to you wanted to enjoy life to the fullest, even if it went against health advice?
    Chapter 3: An Unexpected Gift
  7. How does Angélica’s unexpected survival affect her children’s outlook?
  8. Why do you think Angélica believes “death got confused and took someone else”?
  9. What would you do if you were given only a short time to live but ended up living much longer? Would it change how you approach life?
    Chapter 4: A Miracle
  10. How does Angélica’s healing impact her family’s faith?
  11. What role does gratitude play in Angélica’s reaction to her miraculous recovery?
  12. What would you do if you experienced something that felt like a miracle? Would it change how you view life or spirituality?
    Chapter 5: A New Mission
  13. How does Angélica’s experience with cancer transform her life purpose?
  14. Why do you think Angélica and her family start helping others in their community?
  15. What would you do if you had a second chance at life? Would you focus more on your personal goals or on helping others?
    Chapter 6: Angélica’s Legacy
  16. What values does Angélica pass down to her children and grandchildren?
  17. How does Angélica define “true wealth”?
  18. What would you do to leave a lasting legacy? What message or value would you want to share with others?
