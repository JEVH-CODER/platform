PREGUNTAS - DEBATE

Preguntas de la historia si así es necesario. Utilizar también las actividades abajo especificadas

Historia de Edith:

Chapter 1: An Ordinary Day

  1. What responsibilities does Edith give Christopher before she leaves?
  2. Why do you think Christopher agrees to his mother’s instructions, even though he enjoys freedom and playing outside?
  3. What would you do if you were left in charge of your siblings for a day? Would you follow the instructions, or would you take a risk?

Chapter 2: Temptation Knocks

  1. Why do you think Christopher's friends are encouraging him to leave the house?
  2. How does Samuel react to Christopher’s decision to go to the beach?
  3. What would you do if your friends pressured you to go somewhere against your parents' wishes? Would you be able to say no?

Chapter 3: Trouble in the Water

  1. Why do Christopher's friends think he’s faking his distress in the water?
  2. Why do the friends choose not to help Christopher and instead run away?
  3. What would you do if you were in Christopher's position, struggling in the water with friends who didn’t take you seriously? How would you feel about those friends afterward?

Chapter 4: The Disappearance

  1. What emotions does Edith experience when she finds out that Christopher has left the house?
  2. Why do you think the other children’s parents deny knowing where Christopher went?
  3. How does the neighbor’s information change Edith’s response? What might she be feeling when she learns Christopher went to the beach?

Chapter 5: The Desperate Search

  1. What emotions do you think Edith experiences during her search for Christopher at the beach?
  2. What would you do if a family member went missing? What steps would you take, and whom would you reach out to for help?
  3. Do you think Edith might have done something differently to prevent Christopher’s disappearance? Why or why not?

Chapter 6: The Unexpected Miracle

  1. How does Christopher survive his ordeal, and who helps him?
  2. Why do you think Christopher doesn’t remember what happened during his time in the jungle?
  3. What would you do if you were Christopher and were found by strangers after being lost for days?

Chapter 7: The Lesson Learned

  1. How does Christopher change after his experience? Why do you think he becomes more responsible?
  2. How might this experience affect how Christopher interacts with his friends in the future?
  3. What would you do if you were reminded of a past mistake every time you saw a specific place? Would it change your behavior or decisions?

Historia de Edith:

Chapter 1: An Ordinary Day

  1. What responsibilities does Edith give Christopher before she leaves?
  2. Why do you think Christopher agrees to his mother’s instructions, even though he enjoys freedom and playing outside?
  3. What would you do if you were left in charge of your siblings for a day? Would you follow the instructions, or would you take a risk?

Chapter 2: Temptation Knocks

  1. Why do you think Christopher's friends are encouraging him to leave the house?
  2. How does Samuel react to Christopher’s decision to go to the beach?
  3. What would you do if your friends pressured you to go somewhere against your parents' wishes? Would you be able to say no?

Chapter 3: Trouble in the Water

  1. Why do Christopher's friends think he’s faking his distress in the water?
  2. Why do the friends choose not to help Christopher and instead run away?
  3. What would you do if you were in Christopher's position, struggling in the water with friends who didn’t take you seriously? How would you feel about those friends afterward?

Chapter 4: The Disappearance

  1. What emotions does Edith experience when she finds out that Christopher has left the house?
  2. Why do you think the other children’s parents deny knowing where Christopher went?
  3. How does the neighbor’s information change Edith’s response? What might she be feeling when she learns Christopher went to the beach?

Chapter 5: The Desperate Search

  1. What emotions do you think Edith experiences during her search for Christopher at the beach?
  2. What would you do if a family member went missing? What steps would you take, and whom would you reach out to for help?
  3. Do you think Edith might have done something differently to prevent Christopher’s disappearance? Why or why not?

Chapter 6: The Unexpected Miracle

  1. How does Christopher survive his ordeal, and who helps him?
  2. Why do you think Christopher doesn’t remember what happened during his time in the jungle?
  3. What would you do if you were Christopher and were found by strangers after being lost for days?

Chapter 7: The Lesson Learned

  1. How does Christopher change after his experience? Why do you think he becomes more responsible?
  2. How might this experience affect how Christopher interacts with his friends in the future?
  3. What would you do if you were reminded of a past mistake every time you saw a specific place? Would it change your behavior or decisions?

Despedidas

Las despedidas deben ser reales. Un estudiante se despide de un compañero y este debe darle una respuesta, despidiéndose también.

Traducción

Frase Ejemplo

Nos vemos luego

See you later, alligator!

¡Nos vemos luego, cocodrilo!

Nos vemos pronto

¡Nos vemos pronto, amigo!
