HISTORIA
Pinky, el gato travieso

pinky is between a rock and a hard place his owner wants to kick him out of the house. Just this morning, he got in trouble. When his owner was at the supermarket buying some things for lunch, pinky sat on the table. Beside him, on the table, there was tuna so pinky ate it. Then he saw a pot in front of him, so he jumped and fell inside the pot that had fish soup in it. The pot wasn’t hot yet. His owner had left the pot on low heat for safety while she was at the supermarket. pinky ate so much that he fell asleep inside the pot. When his owner came home, she put the pot on the high heat and started looking for pinky, but he was well hidden—he was inside the pot. After a few minutes, his owner heard the pot moving violently. Could it be that a fish was alive? pinky’s owner uncovered the pot, and something furry shot out. It was pinky, who was terrified and hid under the table. His owner tried to get him out from under the table with a broom, but pinky climbed up her face and hid behind the stove in front of the fridge. However, he was too near the hot pot, so hid beside the cold fridge. When his owner was gonna reach him he jumped, went outside, and scrambled up a tree. pinky is still outside in the tree. He doesn't want to come down, but he is very hungry, so he’s still between a rock and a hard place.

PREGUNTAS - DEBATE
🐾 Dilema 1: ¿Quién tuvo la culpa?

Situación: Pinky comió el almuerzo, se metió en la olla y causó un desastre. Pero su dueña lo dejó solo con la comida y la olla encendida.
Pregunta para debatir:
👉 Who was wrong? Pinky or his owner? Why?

Guía para los estudiantes:

  • Usa frases con "He ate", "She left", "He jumped", "She forgot", "He was hungry", "She was careless", etc.

  • Expresa tu opinión: “I think Pinky was…” / “In my opinion, the owner should…”
